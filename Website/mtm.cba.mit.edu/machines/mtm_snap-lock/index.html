      work volume: 6" x 4" x 1.75"

      leadscrew: 1/4" - 10

      stepper motors: Bipolar NEMA 17

      spindle motor: DC motor (up to 20k rpm)

      stepper motor driver: Allegro A3982

      applications (not limited to): PCB milling, mold making
